Zaira Restaurant

Welcome to Zaira Restaurant, where delicious flavors, elegant ambiance, and warm hospitality come together to create unforgettable dining experiences. Founded in 2020 by Shakil Ahamed M, Zaira has quickly become a favorite destination for families, friends, and food lovers across Tiruvallur and Sriperumbudur.

At Zaira, we take pride in offering more than just great food. With spacious seating for over 130 guests, our restaurant is perfect for casual lunches, family dinners, or weekend get-togethers. For those special moments, both our branches are equipped with dedicated party halls that can accommodate 150+ guests, ideal for birthday parties, receptions, anniversaries, and other celebrations.

Whether you're stopping by for a hearty meal or hosting a grand event, Zaira Restaurant provides the perfect setting and service to make every visit memorable.

Come, celebrate flavor. Celebrate moments. Celebrate with Zaira.

Our Mission

To help our customers enjoy simple edible pleasures and reach their hearts through their stomachs.

Our Vision

To create an ultimate destination for every food lover in town and lead them to an extraordinary food journey.
